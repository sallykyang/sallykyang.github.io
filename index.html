I'm

LSE | Oxford | Compass Lexecon

Hello! I’m Sally Yang.

I’m an MPhil in Economics student at Oxford, with research interests in industrial organisation, competition and applied econometrics.

I kicked off my academic journey with a BSc in Econometrics and Mathematical Economics from LSE, followed by a stint as a research assistant at STICERD. Between 2022 and 2024, I worked as a research analyst at Compass Lexecon, where I worked on real-world competition cases and a bit of discrete choice modelling.

When I’m not crunching numbers, you’ll find me dabbling in graphic design, vibing to K-pop and J-pop, exploring Teyvat, or catching a good play.

This site is a bit of a mishmash right now, but I’m plotting to fill it with something a bit more exciting soon!
